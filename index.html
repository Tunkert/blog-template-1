Why Does this Website Look Like it's the 90s

I was watching a YouTube video by Luke Smith (of LukeSmith.xyz) and he was going on a rant about CSS. He made a very good point I agree with, the websites with the fanciest stuff tend to have the least useful information. I tend to have a theory on that - the websites that spend the least time on the fancy stuff have more time to spend on useful information.
